A collection of schooling institutions across the continent. The central building with the most resources is located in [[Omthes]]. 

The Academy specializes in training and preparing mages of all elements to go into the world and achieve greatness. Although this training typically comes at a cost, either financial or contribution and working for The Academy for a period of time.

There are times where The Academy seeks out individuals and trains them with little to none strings attached. The most common example of this is when a new [[Elemental Magus]] is discovered.
