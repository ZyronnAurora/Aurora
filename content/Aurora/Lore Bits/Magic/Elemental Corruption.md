 Elemental Corruption is a disease that typically affects non-casters and inexperienced mages. It is the result of Primordial Elemental Magic lingering within one's Soul Channels. Each of the six primordial elements causes different effects but all of them can eventually lead to death if too much corruption is absorbed. 


Fire Corruption leads to high fevers and extreme malnutrition as the fire within the soul begins to rapidly consume the body leaving nothing but ash.
 
Life Corruption leads to uncontrolled mutations and in the worst of cases it may cause [[The Chimeric Plague]].
 
Lightning Corruption leads to spasms and uncontrolled thoughts, those affected with this kind of corruption tend to lose their mind before their life.

Stone Corruptions leads to the bones fusing together and causing joints to be unable to move eventually freezing the person in place as their skin dries into stone.

Water Corruption leads to the joints weakening to the point where one cannot stand or move. Eventually the bones themselves erode.

Wind Corruption leads to shortness of breath and asphyxiation as the entire body begins to rapidly absorb all of the air breathed in until eventually no air is enough.
