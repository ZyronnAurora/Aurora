The Elemental Magus is a rare individual who is able to channel each of the primordial elements into their soul without experiencing any [[Elemental Corruption]]. While some casters may be able to channel one or two of the primordial elements, only a Magus is able to use all six. Typically there is one magus born on average every 200 years. There are some times where no such individual exists and the highest recorded quantity of Magus was three approximately 400 years ago.
