Magic works differently from how it typically works in a world of fantasy. Magic is cast and used by opening one's [[Soul]] channels and bringing in a fraction of the soul of a primordial, then using that energy to preform the miracle of magic, then purging one's soul channels of said primordial. This last step is perhaps one of the most important of them all for if a mage does not purge or is unable to, they shall begin to succumb to [[Elemental Corruption]].
