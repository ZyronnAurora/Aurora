A soul is a very unique thing in the world of Aurora, it acts as a protective layer between the body and mind, and the magic of the outside world. So long as the soul barrier is up, most basic types of magic can be shrugged off without much worry. This is also why Earth mages can't crush your bones and Water mages can't pull the blood out of your body.

In order for magic to be cast, the soul must allow part of the soul of a Primordial to enter into their body, they then channel that fraction of the soul to command the respective element to obey. The soul however is a fragile thing from within and begins to be corrupted by the power and might of the Primordial's soul, leading to [[Elemental Corruption]]
