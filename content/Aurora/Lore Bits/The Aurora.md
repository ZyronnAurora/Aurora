 A massive storm of the Six Primal elements. The storm has been raging for all long as recorded history has existed. Each of the six regions of the storm has transformed what was once believed to be a simple plains, into desolate landscapes reforged by their respective element. Nearby and within the outer edges of The Aurora, ancient ruins have been found presenting the idea that the storm could potentially have been created by mortal beings. 

[[Water]] has transformed it's landscape into a constantly storming ocean. No vessel has ever returned from a trip into the storm once they were no longer in vision from the shore. It is said that this region of the storm embodies [[Water]]'s rage and power as they battled against the eldritch threat that led to the end of their life. There are few islands that exist within the storming ocean but as the years pass by, each of them fades to erosion as seldom few remain. Once you pass outside of The Aurora, the ocean becomes gentle rivers creating excellent farmland and plant growth permitting civilization to thrive.

[[Wind]] is the most inviting of all of the regions but this façade has lead to the death of many of those who tried to peer deeper into The Aurora. Once you pass into the storm within [[Wind]]'s domain, the breeze gentle guides you deeper until it throws the trusting adventurer into the air and slams them against the ground. Tornados suddenly appear from no where and any shelter one might have would be torn up from it's root in the face of this overwhelming force. Outside of The Aurora, the breeze is constant and has permitted harnessing of this constant [[wind]] to produce miraculous feats of physical engineering.

[[Stone]] presents itself with no trickery or illusion of what it is. This area is a mighty mountain with caves and ridges that at any glance would present their danger. Those who brave the ridge might attempt their best but one misplaced foot and a fall is all that awaits you. The caves are no better, with no clear path and no light, anyone who dares to explore them is lost searching for their way back until their resources run dry and they succumb to death. The region is known for its earthquakes and there are rumors that despite the evidence, the caves shift as soon as anyone enters them. The area outside of The Aurora is blessed with rich resources and a hilly landscape where fortune might await anyone who visits.

[[Fire]]**'s influence is much like its temperament, burning twice as bright and half as long, but still presenting the most danger to those who dare contest their might. The area within the storm is full of volcanos and rivers of lava. The heat melts the armor of any of those who dare approach, that is if they are even able to get that far. The area outside of the Aurora is not safe from [[Fire]]'s influence, the landscape had become a desert nearly as barren as any part of the storm. Those who explore the desert however might find relics that are infused with [[Fire]]'s power, along with potential infused threats.

**[[Lightning]]** is unpredictable as a region but the one with the most potential. As you explore the region, the lighting will flash in the distance and so long as you do not have any metal on you it will tend to leave you alone, lulling you into a false sense of security. This is until it strikes, not leaving a moment to regret one's choices. The area outside of The Aurora has used the power of [[lightning]] to create powerful tools using the electricity.

**[[Life]]** exists as a dense jungle with creatures that have grown far past what nature had intended. These beasts consume all that enter the region with a viscous hunger. Those who survive long enough in this region of The Storm eventually fall to it's influence and succumb to [[The Chimeric Plague]], a corruption of their mind and energy. [[Life]] while the most beneficial of all the magics must be carefully controlled. Outside of The Aurora, life blossoms as bountiful harvests and cattle exist, although the occasional Chimeric Beast leaves The Aurora and threatens the peaceful life of those nearby.


Regions where the two elements border along each other carry traits of both, leading to extreme bounties but extreme dangers.

The calendar is based around the estimated formation date of the Aurora, therefore the Aurora was formed on 00/01/01
