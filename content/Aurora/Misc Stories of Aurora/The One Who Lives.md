Log 1:
My name is Norman Richards, and this is the beginning of my study into a figure of mystery and legend. From what information I gather, this being seems to appear in the moments before disaster. My current theory is that they might be a higher being who is tasked with witnessing the deaths of mortals and passing their souls on to the afterlife. The only issue with this is that there is no information about this being. You would think that someone would have gathered information and documented this being like many before but there is a suspicious lack of that information. I am currently on route to the most recent disaster sight to see if there are any signs of who or whatever this being might be.

Log 2:
It has been a week since my last entry. I arrived at Renticul where [[The Chimeric Plague]] killed all the residence of this village. Unfortunately there were no survivors and the area has been completely abandoned, with the exception of myself. During this time I found a journal written by a shopkeeper who seems to have met this being. The final entry reads as such

`"I opened my shop as normal today when this sickly looking gentleman approached. There was a sadness in his eyes and when I asked him what is wrong, he told me that it didn't matter. I wouldn't be there to do anything about it. That spooked me half to death and I was close to calling the guards but he ended showing off his wealthy pockets so I served him up some goods and got me a generous tip. Drinks are on me tonight and The Rat's Bar is going to run dry." - Unknown Author in a Journal Dated the Night Before The Chimeric Massacure of Renticul`
